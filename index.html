Information About me

I was born in La Paz Bolivia.

I completed my undergraduate degree in 2015 at the National University of San Andres, Bolivia, where I earned a Bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering. In 2018, I earned a Master's degree from Yamagata University's Graduate School of Science and Engineering, with a focus on Management of Technology. My research thesis was highly evaluated by the academic professors, resulting in my being honored with an award for my work. This experience provided me with a deep understanding of the importance of hard work, dedication, and attention to detail in achieving my academic and professional goals.

After completing my studies at San Andres University, I began my career at COFAR S.A. in September 2015. During my time in the IT Department, I gained experience working with databases, virtual machines, and software installation, among other skills. After a year, I left to pursue further education in Yamagata Prefecture, Japan. Upon completing my master studies, I joined Suzuki High-Tech Corporation in October 2018, where I worked in the development department and also took on project management responsibilities. In December 2021, I joined Aillumission, where I was assigned to the ICT Solutions Department, working on the Earthquake and Tsunami Warning System application for Rakuten Mobile. In this role, I utilized my front-end development skills and also collaborated with the QA testing team.

In terms of my personality, I consider myself to be an extremely curious individual, and I am always eager to learn new things. I am also a flexible person, capable of adapting to new situations and working effectively in teams. I take great pride in collaborating with others to achieve common goals, and I have demonstrated an ability to remain composed and responsive under challenging circumstances. I am fluent in 3 languages, including Spanish as my mother tongue, and English and Japanese for business communication. This linguistic versatility has proven to be a valuable asset throughout my academic and professional career, allowing me to engage with a broader range of cultures and perspectives.
